House Rules
🚭
No Smoking
Strictly prohibited indoors, in bathrooms, and in the courtyard. Minimum USD 500 penalty.
🔇
Quiet Hours · 9 PM – 9 AM
Please keep noise down, especially in the courtyard when coming and going late at night.
👥
Registered Guests Only
Only guests in the reservation may stay overnight. Additional visitors must be pre-approved.
🧼
Spills & Damage
Please wipe up spills immediately to avoid permanent stains. Let us know if anything was accidentally damaged.
🚽
Toilet
Flush toilet paper only. Do not flush wet wipes, tissues, or sanitary products — clogging may result in repair costs charged to the guest.
🗑️
Trash
Seal all trash bags and place by the sidewalk near the trees around 7 PM. Collection: Tue, Thu & Sun nights. See the Recycling Guide for sorting rules.
Cautions
🚿
Bathroom Drain
The shared bathroom drain is quite small — please avoid leaving the water running for a long time, as it may overflow.
👣
Mind Your Step
The floor height in the 2nd-floor hallway varies — please watch your step. Also be careful of low clearance when using the internal stairs.
🌿
Insects & Nature
Surrounded by forest, small insects may enter through gaps. Keep screen windows closed when not in use or when leaving.
⚠️
Emergency Numbers
Fire extinguishers are located throughout the house — please find them when you arrive.
Fire & Ambulance 119  ·  Police 112
