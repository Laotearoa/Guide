Troubleshooting
Code not working after entering correctly
Make sure you complete the wake sequence first (touch with multiple fingers, then tap the two random numbers and the star). The code won't work if you skip this.
🔢
Wrong code entered multiple times
The lock will temporarily disable and sound an alarm. Do not press anything. Wait a few minutes for it to reset, then start again from the top.
⏱️
Door feels stuck after unlocking
Hanok wooden doors can swell with humidity. Try pushing the door gently flush against the frame while pulling the handle. If it doesn't open, message us on Airbnb immediately.
🚪
Keypad won't light up at all
The battery may be discharged. Please message us on Airbnb right away and we will resolve it as quickly as possible.
🔋
Still can't get in
Message us on Airbnb — we will get back to you as soon as we see your message and sort it out as quickly as possible.
