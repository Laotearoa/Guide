House Rules

No Smoking
Strictly prohibited indoors, in bathrooms, and in the courtyard. Minimum USD 500 penalty.
🔇
Quiet Hours · 9 PM – 9 AM
Please keep noise down especially in the courtyard when coming and going.
👥
Registered Guests Only
Only guests in the reservation may stay overnight. Additional visitors must be pre-approved.
🧼
Spills & Damage
Please wipe up spills immediately to avoid permanent stains. Let us know if anything was accidentally damaged.
🚽
Toilet Use
Flush toilet paper only. Do not flush wet wipes, tissues, or sanitary products. Clogging from improper disposal may result in repair costs.
🔑
Door Lock
Before heading out, turn off all lights and close all mosquito nets and doors. Hanok doors may warp — double-check the lock each time.
